Welcome

Welcome to the Department of Civil Engineering at the National Taipei University of Technology.

The Department of Civil Engineering is devoted to offering professional knowledge and techniques in the planning, investigation, design, testing, drawing, construction, examination and maintenance management of civil engineering related projects.

The future focus of the education and research in the department will be placed continuously on the development of structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, construction management, ecological engineering, information technology, and water resource.
